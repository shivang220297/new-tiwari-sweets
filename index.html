Humaari Kahani

Raj Dev Tiwari —
Ek Naam, Ek Bharosa

1968 mein ek chhota sa sapna leke nikle the Raj Dev ji — aur aaj Kanpur ke Jajmau mein unka naam meethai ka paryaay ban gaya hai. Late 60s mein bhi woh young dil se jeete hain. Dukaan mein qaddam rakho — woh khud milenge, hansenge, aur agar quality mein kami hui toh seedha bol denge. Koi filter nahin, koi nautanki nahin. Yehi hai Raj Dev ji ka asli andaaz.

Holi ho ya Diwali, woh sirf apne ghar mein nahin manate — poore mohalle ke saath manate hain. Tyohaar unke liye sirf business nahin, dil ka mamla hai. Ab unke bete ne dukaan ki zimmedaari sambhaal li hai — corporate background se naye ideas laata hai, experiment karta hai — lekin purani rooh waisi hi rehti hai.
